How we met

We met through mutual friends in 2019 when I was studying for my Masters in Toronto. Nandhitha surprised me with a no-nonsense invite to go grocery shopping—no introduction, no explanation, just straight to the point! From that moment, we clicked and quickly became best friends, sharing life’s little moments like thesis deadlines, music, taxes, and the comfort of a new friendship in a new country. Together, we faced all kinds of challenges, from pandemic life to job searches and house-hunting, always finding joy in navigating them side by side. Five years, countless memories, and a lifetime ahead—we’re so glad for that first grocery run!
