How we met

We met through mutual friends while pursuing our master’s degrees at the University of Toronto—a serendipitous beginning, that turned even sweeter when we later, coincidentally, became neighbors. As our friend groups naturally blended, so did our lives. Together, we tackled late-night thesis deadlines, encouraged each other through nerve-wracking defenses, and cheered for every milestone along the way.
When the world slowed down during the pandemic, we leaned on each other, finding comfort and strength in the small things: grocery store runs and socially distanced check-ins- which after lead to shared playlists, movies, spontaneous trips. We also shared the occasional frustration over job searches, rents and finances, personal and professional struggles. Every challenge became a stepping stone, every joy a thread weaving us closer together.
Looking back now, it feels like destiny brought us together—from our first impromptu grocery run to countless adventures that followed. Here’s to always finding joy in navigating life side by side and to a lifetime of love and laughter ahead!

Little Flower Church 4:00PM to 5:30PM (Wedding)

On May 29th, 2025, at Little Flower Church in Piravom, we'll be getting married, the destination of our beautiful journey together. Returning to this special place to take such an important step, holds deep meaning for him, and it would be magical to celebrate this milestone surrounded by the most important people in his life — his family, relatives, and cherished friends from school and college. The ceremony will include traditional vows, blessings, rings, and a reception after.
